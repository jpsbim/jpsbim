BIM Services offered

Mechanical Modelling

Mechanical BIM modelling services include creation and use of coordinated, consistent computable information about a building's mechanical design. Our mechanical BIM models follow specified standards to ensure certainty of geometry, work flows and scheduling.

  • Heating, Ventilation, Air-Conditioning(HVAC) Modelling
  • Mechanical Duct and Duct Fittings Modelling
  • Mechanical Equipment Modelling as per submittal/cut-sheet provided
  • Duct Layout as per project specification and code applicable
  • BOQ of Ducts, Duct Fittings, Mechanical Equipment, Diffusers, Grills, etc.
  • Mechanical Equipment Detailing (sections and isometric)
  • Mechanical Components Modelling (with connections)
  • Walk-Through of MEP / BIM Models
  • Rendered views of Mechanical BIM Models
  • Hydronic pipe sizing, routing
  • Terminal units part modelling
  • Revit family creation
  • AHU, RTU, MAU systems
  • Pump head calc, duct pr. drop calc
  • Trace & eQuest energy calculation and simulation
  • Coordinated 3D Piping Models
  • Mechanical Piping Shop Drawings
  • Mechanical Room Layout Models
  • Fire Protection Models
  • Sleeve / Penetration Drawings

Electrial Modelling

Creating the electrical model of all the conduits, tray layouts, lighting fixtures & fire alarm locations from the design documents & cut sheets.

  • Electrical Lighting Fixtures Layout Modelling as per given schedules
  • Detailed light fixtures with including : volts, lamps, fitting type and Parameters
  • Wiring of fixtures with switches and panels as per drawing and Atouatary regulation
  • Cable trays in BIM Electrical Model as per given drawings
  • Rendering of BIM Model with Lights
  • Walk through of Electrical BIM Model
  • BOQ of Electrical Elements
  • Schedules of circuits and panel boards
  • Electrical Single Line Diagrams
  • Electrical, Power and Lighting System plans
  • Electrical Design and Drafting Services
  • Design and Drafting of Lighting, Power Systems
  • Electrical Power and Lighting System Plans
  • Access Control/ CCTV/ Telecommunication/ System Integration/ PA Systems
